WGA Overwatch

About Us

WGA Overwatch is a registered student organization under the University of Washington, Seattle and is sponsored by Tespa Esports. As a student gaming association, we strive to connect students from various backgrounds and communities with one common interest, Overwatch. Even though we are all connected by Overwatch, we are still a group of friends that go out and play other games together. Occasionally we even play games such as Osu! and Minecraft! We are always looking to make more friends!

What is Overwatch?

Overwatch is team-based multiplayer first-person shooter game that was developed and published by Blizzard Entertainment. In the game, players are organized into two teams of six and each player selects a character from a roster of "heroes" to play as. Teams are to work together to secure or defend control points or escort a payload across the map. Join up with your friends and play for fun or test your skills against other players in the competitive mode.
