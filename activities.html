Activities

Things We Do!

Join us in our weekly custom games! We play a variety of different types of games such as 6v6's, 12 person deathmatches, elimination, and sometimes originally created game modes. Add us in game to join in on our weekly fun!

We have quarterly events where we gather our members, get together, and hang out! These events are often local tournaments and viewing parties for official tournaments! Find and get to know us at one of our events or check us out at annual gaming events like the Husky Gaming Expo and Hub Game Crawl!

Watch out for special giveaways that we might have during our events! All you have to do is show up and participate in our activities to have a chance to win one of our many Overwatch related prizes!

Looking for people to play with? Want to climb the Overwatch ranks? Message our Discord Server with your Blizzard ID and current rank to find others around the same rank to play with! We have many people in all different ranks always looking for new people to play with!

Competitive

Are you interested in competitive play? Want to join a competitive team and test your skills against other collegiate teams? Join one of our UW Overwatch teams today! Currently we have an A Team and a B Team playing for us!

  • A Team Requirement:
    • Currently ranked Masters or above
  • B Team Requirement:
    • Currently ranked Diamond

If you are interested in playing for one of our UW Overwatch teams and meet the above requirements, speak to our team captain Will Nguyen(aka Wizediablo).

If you currently do not meet our requirements but am still interested in joining our competitive teams, rise up the ranks! Find others who also want to climb higher and queue up with them! Ask people on our team for tips and tricks on how to do better and win more often!

Our teams often do skirmishes to practice, either in-house(against each other) or against other collegiate teams! If this sounds like something you want to do, join our skirmishes to see how you fare with our team and against other teams!
